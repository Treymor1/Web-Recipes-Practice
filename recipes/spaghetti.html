Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a long, thin, solid, cylindrical pasta. It is a staple food of traditional Italian cuisine.

Ingredents

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 (6ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup
  • 2 1/2 cups water
